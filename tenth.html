1 Display employee name, hire date, and current date. Also calculate the number of days the employee has worked. EMP (ENAME, HIREDATE)
Show Answer
SELECT ENAME, HIREDATE, SYSDATE, ROUND(SYSDATE - HIREDATE) AS DAYS_WORKED FROM EMP;
