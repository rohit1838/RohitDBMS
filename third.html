1 Delete employees earning less than the average salary.

Show Answer
DELETE FROM EMP WHERE DEPTNO IN ( SELECT DEPTNO FROM EMP GROUP BY DEPTNO HAVING COUNT(*) < 3 );

5 Delete employees whose salary equals the minimum salary of their department.
Show Answer
